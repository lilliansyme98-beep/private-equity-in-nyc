Methodology

To identify PE-backed restaurants in New York City, this story cross-referenced confirmed private equity deals from PE firm portfolio pages, press releases, and trade press (Nation's Restaurant News, Restaurant Business) against the NYC Department of Health restaurant inspection database (downloaded May 10, 2026), which covers all food establishments in the five boroughs regardless of alcohol service.

Deals were verified through primary sources including PE firm announcements and press releases filed with PR Newswire and Business Wire. Deal amounts are listed where publicly disclosed; the majority of transactions were not disclosed publicly. A restaurant group was included only when a PE deal could be confirmed through at least one primary source.

Four false positives were removed from the DOH crossref: Russian restaurants whose names include the suffix "-naya" that matched the search term for Naya Group.

The NYC Liquor Authority active license database (April 14, 2026) was used as a secondary source to illustrate LLC ownership opacity and was not used as the primary methodology, as it covers only establishments with active liquor licenses.

Addresses for the map were geocoded using the U.S. Census Bureau Geocoder batch API. Eight addresses (primarily airport terminal and incomplete locations) could not be geocoded and were excluded from the map.
